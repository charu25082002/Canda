Our Rich Heritage in Puducherry

Canda Gold Craft & Jewellery - Puducherry's Trusted Jeweller Since 1931

Canda Gold Craft & Jewellery has a rich legacy that began in 1931 when Mr. Ramu Achari entered the jewellery industry in Puducherry with deep passion and skilled craftsmanship inherited from his elders. He soon earned a strong reputation for creating exceptional jewellery, especially in Sri Aurobindo and Mother designs.

He is remembered for crafting the Mother's crown with diamonds and rubies, as well as contributing to the making of the Sacred Symbol of Mother & Sri Aurobindo. Over the generations, the family continued training artisans and today operates with highly skilled staff in a home-based workshop in Puducherry.
