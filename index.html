Our Rich Heritage

Canda Gold Craft & Jewellery

Canda Gold Craft & Jewellery has a rich legacy that began in 1931 when Mr. Ramu Achari entered the jewellery industry with deep passion and skilled craftsmanship inherited from his elders. He soon earned a strong reputation for creating exceptional jewellery, especially in Sri Aurobindo and Mother designs.

He is remembered for crafting the Mother's crown with diamonds and rubies, as well as contributing to the making of the Sacred Symbol of Mother & Sri Aurobindo. Over the generations, the family continued training artisans and today operates with highly skilled staff in a home-based workshop.

Continuing the Legacy

Inspired by his grandfather, the current generation began learning jewellery-making at the young age of 13 and pursued the craft professionally from 1995 and 2008 onwards, gaining deep expertise and passion.

Today, Canda Gold Craft upholds the core values of quality, service, and punctuality, and continues to lead in Mother Gold jewellery in Pondicherry.
