Our Story

From Humble Beginnings to Jewelry Excellence

In 1985, Mr. Chandrashekhar Canda started a small jewelry workshop in Mumbai with nothing but a dream and exceptional craftsmanship skills. What began as a modest family business has now blossomed into one of Mumbai's most trusted names in gold and silver jewelry.

1985

The Beginning

Mr. Chandrashekhar Canda established a small workshop in Mumbai, focusing on traditional gold jewelry craftsmanship.

1995

First Showroom

Opened our first retail showroom in Mumbai, introducing the "Canda Gold Craft & Jewellery" brand to customers.

2005

Expansion

Expanded our collection to include sterling silver jewelry and introduced the "MotherGold" heritage collection.

2015

New Generation

Third generation joined the business, bringing modern designs while preserving traditional craftsmanship.

2023

Present Day

Now serving thousands of happy customers with three generations of expertise and trust.

"Jewelry is not just an ornament; it's a story, a memory, and a legacy that lasts for generations."

- Mr. Chandrashekhar Canda, Founder
Master Artisans

Our Craftsmanship

Each piece of jewelry at Canda Gold Craft undergoes a meticulous process that combines traditional techniques with modern precision.

01

Design & Conceptualization

Every design begins with sketches and consultations, ensuring it meets both aesthetic and functional requirements.

02

Material Selection

Only BIS hallmarked 22K/24K gold and 925 sterling silver are used, along with certified precious stones.

03

Handcrafting

Master artisans handcraft each piece using traditional techniques like filigree, meenakari, and kundan work.

04

Quality Check

Every piece undergoes rigorous quality checks for weight, purity, finishing, and durability.
