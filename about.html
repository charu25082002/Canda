Our Story in Puducherry

A Legacy of Gold Jewellery Excellence in Puducherry Since 1931

In 1931, Mr. Ramu Achari entered the jewellery industry in Puducherry with deep passion and skilled craftsmanship inherited from his elders. He soon earned a strong reputation as Puducherry's trusted gold jeweller, creating exceptional gold ornaments, especially in Sri Aurobindo and Mother designs. He is remembered for crafting the Mother's crown with diamonds and rubies, as well as contributing to the making of the Sacred Symbol of Mother & Sri Aurobindo.
